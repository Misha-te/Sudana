How to create a Sudana account

  1. Open the signup page.
  2. Enter the required information.
  3. Provide an email address or phone number.
  4. Enter the verification code.
  5. Complete account creation.
  6. Log in.

How to report a problem

  1. Go to the page where the problem occurred.
  2. Take a screenshot when possible.
  3. Open the main menu.
  4. Select “Support from our team.”
  5. Select “Report a problem.”
  6. Describe what happened.
  7. Upload the screenshot.
  8. Submit the report.
